The Podcast About Chain Restaurants

Nick Wiger

Nicholas F “Nick” Wiger is an American comedian, improviser, podcast personality, and television writer. He is the co-founder and co-host of the popular weekly podcast Doughboys, which reviews chain restaurants with guests and includes additional segments centered around snack food and related topics.

Mike Mitchell

Michael Donovan "Mitch" "Spoonman a.k.a Mr. Slice" Mitchell is an American actor, comedian, and writer best known as a member of The Birthday Boys sketch comedy group. He is also known for his role as Randy Monahan on the Netflix series Love.
